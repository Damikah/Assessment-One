Infinity Nikki

Infinity Nikki is a cozy open world game that was developed by the company Infold Games. It is the fifth installement in the Nikki Game Series, following: Nikki UP2U, Hello Nikki, Love Nikki-Dress UP Queen and Shining Nikki. This multi-platform game includes, puzzle-solving, dress up, and platforming using ones abilities and other mechanics in the game for an exceptional and fresh experience.
